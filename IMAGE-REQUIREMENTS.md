## 🎯 Pro Tips

1. **Image Quality:** Use high-resolution images (min 800px width)
2. **File Format:** JPEG for photos (.jpg), PNG for graphics with transparency (.png)
3. **File Size:** Optimize images (keep under 500KB each for faster loading)
4. **Naming Convention:** Use lowercase, hyphens (not spaces)
5. **Before/After:** Can be single split images OR separate images (we can adjust code)
